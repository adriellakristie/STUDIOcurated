TEDX PASARBARU

A TEDx event is a local gathering where live TED-like talks and performances are shared with the community. TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. A TEDx talk is a showcase for speakers presenting great, well-formed ideas in under 18 minutes. From tech demonstrations to dance performances, a TEDx talk introduces exciting new ideas and developments, and presents old ones with a new perspective.
