Self Unmasked is a youth-led organization looking to provide and increase social awareness on mental health. I designed Instagram Feed Posts, Stories and Videos as well. Scroll down to see are my works.

WORKS

MEANING BEHIND THE LOGO

Our logo represents members of the society that are often afraid to speak up on their struggles. As mental health is often wrongly viewed as shameful, people in present times tend to mask over what is truly felt. It is a frequent occasion where emotions splayed on the surface of faces aren't accurate to what is genuinely felt within.
