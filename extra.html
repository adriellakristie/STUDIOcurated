JAKARTA EMAS

Born and raised in Jakarta, I’ve witnessed firsthand the city’s rapid transformation. As Indonesia approaches its Emas 2045 vision, I created a piece titled Jakarta Emas to reflect on this shift. While economic progress and industrial growth are celebrated, I wanted to highlight what we might be sacrificing in the process. Known for its vast natural resources, Indonesia is now seeing Jakarta’s landscape dominated by skyscrapers, private vehicles, and gray skies. Grasslands give way to concrete roads, and vibrant flowers fade, not with time, but as a consequence of our own actions.
