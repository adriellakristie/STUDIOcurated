RCS CUP

RCS CUP is an annual inter-school competition event held by Raffles Christian School Pondok Indah. I worked for RCS CUP as a paid freelance designer.

WORKS

LOGO

Illustrated in Procreate, this logo was designed to embody the event’s slogan, “Chasing Dreams, Winning Reality.” The knight figure was chosen as a symbol of ambition, strength, and perseverance, making it a fitting representation for the competition.

POSTERS

Instagram Story

This graphic was created in Procreate for the event’s Instagram story, using layering, custom brushes, and hand-drawn elements to highlight key event details in a clear and visually engaging way.
