SLOW MOVE BAZAAR

Slow Move Bazaar is Indonesia's first and biggest sustainable bazaar in Indonesia, with 137,000 followers on Instagram. As a graphic designer in Slow Move Bazaar, I worked on creating a range of visuals, contributing to promotional materials and set designs. I created cohesive visual elements and assets for use across designs, as well as posters and social media graphics to effectively engage the audience and promote the event. I also designed props and set elements that helped bring the bazaar’s theme to life, enhancing the overall visual experience.
