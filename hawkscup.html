HAWKS CUP

HawksCup is an annual sports, academics and arts event held by Global Jaya School. The theme for HawksCup 2023 was Silver Screen Showdown, a take on the infamous hollywood scene. I worked as head of publications, covering all aspects regarding media and design.

WORKS

LOGO

This logo was illustrated for the event Hawks Cup. Inspired by the theme ‘Silver Screen Showdown’, I incorporated design elements reminiscent of classic Hollywood. I incorporated marquee lights, theater signage, and cinematic motifs. Techniques such as layering, gradient shading, and custom brushes were used to create depth, highlights, and a polished retro look. The bold typography and star accents reinforce the celebratory, competitive spirit of the event.
